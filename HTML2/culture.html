  OUR WORK CULTURE

At Cube26, we don’t just work. Instead, we challenge ourselves to bring out the best in each other as a team, together. While our workforce is majorly a mix of young millennials and old-school yet futuristic leaders, the organizational aim has always been constant and unidirectional - to be the best at

developing futuristic technology, unanimously.

 

  Easy Going yet proactive

While there aren’t many designative restraints, there is an acquiescent sense of freedom at work. To ensure that the work we do is quality and always on time, we’ve made sure that the office timings are flexible, relieving the employees from a potential distraction. Not to mention, such a practice often encourages the minds at work to finish their deliverables at the earliest, without any compromise on the quality whatsoever. Having said that, we never back down to give our fullest at work. When the need is to work extra hours, we never shy back.
